The other day, when I blogged A long last, victorious we were, I got one of the details wrong.

As mistakes go, it was a pretty serious one.

Remember when I was asked when it started, and I said I wasn't sure, but I thought it was from the date of the email?

I shouldn't have guessed.

I don't know why I did?

Because that guess was wrong.

Really, really wrong.

Basically, all "GIVE" contributions on or after January 1st are eligible!

I know of several people who will benefit from that difference.

Including one person who just left Microsoft!
